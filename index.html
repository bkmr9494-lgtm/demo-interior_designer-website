FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for an interior design project?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room makeover typically takes 3-4 weeks, while a complete home interior can range from 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

What is the cost range for interior design services?

Our projects typically range from ₹5 lakhs to ₹50 lakhs+ depending on the scope, materials, and finishes selected. We work with various budgets and provide transparent pricing from the start.

Do you provide 3D visualizations before execution?

Yes! We provide detailed 3D renders and virtual walkthroughs for every project. This allows you to visualize your space before any construction begins and make changes as needed.

What is included in your turnkey solutions?

Our turnkey solutions include complete project management from design to handover—concept design, 3D visualization, material procurement, civil work, electrical, plumbing, furniture, décor, and final styling.

Do you work on commercial projects?

Absolutely! We design offices, retail spaces, restaurants, hotels, and other commercial establishments. Our commercial designs focus on functionality, brand identity, and creating memorable customer experiences.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Delhi NCR including Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. For premium projects, we also work in other cities across India.
